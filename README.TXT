PROJECT TITLE: Shadow's Below
VERSION or DATE: June 2025
AUTHORS: Chin-En, Jaclyn, Elise, Yuvia, Julia


--- FEATURES ---

Endless score-based survival horror game - make your way through the mazes, but beware of the monsters that lurk within! Features include...
- A neat instructions screen
- Random maze generation (written by Mr. Cohen)
- Dark lighting effect, with ring of light around player
- Scary ambient sounds
- High risk high reward aspect - as you progress through the maze levels, coins give you increasingly more XP, but enemies get stronger too
- Enemy pathfinding
- Turn-based gameplay - for every block that player moves, enemy moves one block too
- Player shooting ability
- Collectible powerups: heart (increases player health), lantern (increases light radius around player)
- A high score save system
- A super spooky jumpscare when you die
